Title: Realm Royale

Rating: 5/10

Ownership: Steam - PC

Hours Played: 5 Hours

Start Date: 6/8/18

Finish Date: 9/12/18

Thoughts:
The addition of classes to the battle royale genre alongside its fable like
art direction took most of the PC world by storm. However, the game ultimately
ended up feeling like most of the other battle royale games and quickly faded
back into obscurity.
